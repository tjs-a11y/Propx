Legal Protection

Non-Circumvention Agreement

Every wholesaler signs this automatically when they submit a deal. Protects you from being cut out of the transaction. One page. Plain language. Enforceable.

NON-CIRCUMVENTION, NON-DISCLOSURE & CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT
PropX Acquisitions LLC · deals@propx.io

This Non-Circumvention, Non-Disclosure and Confidentiality Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the date of deal submission between PropX Acquisitions LLC ("PropX") and the submitting party identified below ("Submitting Party").

1. Purpose

The Submitting Party is providing PropX with information about one or more real property opportunities ("Deal(s)") for the purpose of PropX sourcing capital, buyers, or financing for such Deal(s). The parties wish to define the terms under which such information is shared and to protect their respective interests.

2. Non-Circumvention

The Submitting Party agrees not to circumvent, avoid, bypass, or obviate PropX's interest in any transaction arising from information submitted through PropX's platform or communicated directly to PropX. The Submitting Party shall not directly contact, solicit, or transact with any buyer, investor, lender, or other party introduced or identified by PropX in connection with any Deal without PropX's prior written consent for a period of 24 months from the date of submission.

3. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to keep confidential all information exchanged in connection with any Deal, including but not limited to buyer identities, offer prices, transaction structures, and closing details. Neither party shall disclose such information to any third party without prior written consent of the other party.

4. PropX's Role

The Submitting Party acknowledges that PropX acts as a principal in all transactions — buying and reselling properties independently. The Submitting Party's compensation is their agreed asking price, which PropX agrees to pay in full at closing. The Submitting Party has no claim to any profit, spread, or additional compensation realized by PropX above the agreed purchase price.

5. Term and Remedies

This Agreement shall remain in effect for 24 months from the date of execution. In the event of a breach, the non-breaching party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief and monetary damages including but not limited to lost profits arising from the circumvented transaction.

6. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the state in which the subject property is located. If multiple properties are involved, the parties agree that Georgia law shall govern unless otherwise specified in writing.
